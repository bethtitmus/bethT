What is VR?

VR or Virtual reality is using a computer to create a simulated enviorment. unlike traditional user interfaces, VR places the user inside of the experience. this makes it feel real for them and they can interact with the 3D worlds. we do this by simulation as many senses as possible. such as vision, hearing, touch, and maybe even smell.

What is AR?

Virtual reality and Augmented reality are two sides of the same coin. This is because augmented reality is similar but involves more of the real world Augmented reality simulates artificial objects into real environments. Augmented reality will normally use a camera to make this possible letting you see computer-generated things in the real world. This is diffrent from the completely computer made enviormennt.
